What are amino acids?

Amino acids play crucial roles in various biological processes, including protein synthesis, enzyme function, and cell signaling. They are essential for the growth, repair, and maintenance of tissues in organisms. Additionally, some amino acids serve as precursors for important molecules like neurotransmitters and nucleotides.
